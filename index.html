About Us

Every Sip is a Story

Imagine stepping into a café where every sip transports you to a different corner of the world. One moment, you're tasting the smoky depths of a Sumatran roast, and the next, you're savoring the citrusy brightness of an Ethiopian blend. At Bean There, Brewed That, coffee isn't just a drink—it’s an adventure waiting to be explored.

Our journey began in a small, bustling street café in Barcelona, where the scent of fresh espresso mixed with the salty Mediterranean air. It was here that the idea for Bean There, Brewed That was born—not just a coffee shop, but a passport to flavors from across the world. We spent years traveling, learning from master roasters, and curating the perfect selection of beans that capture the essence of every place we've been. From the delicate floral notes of Ethiopian Yirgacheffe to the bold, chocolatey richness of Colombian beans, we bring the world to your cup.

But our café is more than just a place to grab coffee—it’s a space for dreamers, travelers, and coffee lovers to gather, share stories, and fuel their next adventure. Whether you're sipping an espresso before a big meeting, catching up with an old friend over a latte, or indulging in a cold brew on a sunny afternoon, you’re not just drinking coffee—you’re embarking on a journey. So sit back, take a sip, and let the flavors take you somewhere new.
